# Digital Assets Ecosystem - Infrastructure & Competitive Intelligence

A competitive intelligence tool mapping the institutional digital assets infrastructure stack. Nine layers. 61 companies. Built to surface the competitive dynamics, ownership structures, and strategic positioning that define how digital assets actually work at an institutional level.

**Layers covered:** Custody & Wallet Management · KYT / Compliance / AML · Trading & Execution · Settlement · Reconciliation · Tokenization & Transfer Agent · Stablecoin Infrastructure · Data & Reporting · Technology & Node Infrastructure

**Features:** Layer-by-layer company directory · Stack Coverage Map showing multi-layer expansion patterns · Company profiles with funding data, regulatory status, and competitive positioning · Live stock pricing for public companies
